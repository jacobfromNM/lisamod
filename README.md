_"Forgive me Lisa, there's a monster inside of me."_

**Versions:** Minecraft 1.19.2 & 1.20.1

**Author:** jacobfromnm

### **Overview**

The Lisa mod introduces a haunting ambient entity inspired by the terrifying ghost from Hideo Kojima's legendary P.T. demo. She lurks silently in the dark, appearing without warning and vanishing just as quickly, echoing the tragic cries of her final moments. This mod pairs nicely with the [Sound Physics Remastered](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/sound-physics-remastered) mod!

### **Features:**

*   Ambient Horror: Lisa doesn't chase you. She simply appears in the dark, at night or deep underground, and lingers nearby, making her presence known through unsettling sounds.
*   If Lisa decides to move, she won't come from in front of you.
*   Untouchable: Lisa is invulnerable. She cannot be fought, interacted with, or reasoned with.
*   Atmospheric Sound Design: Featuring a range of haunting ambient, vocal, and appearance sounds from the original demo that build dread before you ever see her.

Configuration file lets you adjust:

*   Spawn chance and cooldown
*   Cave and night spawning
*   Phase timing (lurk delay, phase 2 linger duration)
*   Damage dealt on eye contact
*   Ambient stalk chance and duration
*   Darkness effect range
*   Flicker effect toggle
*   Debug logging

Feedback/Suggestions are welcome - thanks for checking it out, I hope it adds to your experience!

### **Warning:**

This mod was designed as a tribute to one of the most iconic horror demos ever made, for players who want that creeping sense of dread added to their Minecraft world. If you're not a fan of unsettling audio/ambiance, jump scares or paranoid glancing over your shoulder, proceed with caution!
